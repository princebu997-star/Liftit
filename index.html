Our Story

LiftIt was born in 2024 when we realized that small business owners — whether running a gym, library, or small company — were struggling with complicated, expensive, or ineffective management software. Some were too costly, some too complex, and some simply didn't work.

So we asked ourselves: why not build a platform that works for everyone, everywhere? Simple, affordable, and accessible to businesses across the globe. Today, thousands of business owners trust LiftIt to manage their operations smoothly.

🏋️ For Gym Owners

Track members, manage fees, create workout plans, monitor attendance — all from one dashboard. No more paper registers or messy spreadsheets.

📚 For Library Owners

Manage books, track students, calculate late fees automatically, and monitor reading progress. Library management has never been this easy.

🏢 For Company Owners

Perfect for small businesses with 15-50 employees. Track attendance, manage salaries, handle payroll, and generate reports with just one click.

👥 For Personal Use

Track family fitness goals, manage daily expenses, monitor health metrics, and keep everyone motivated. A healthier family, a happier life.
